The Golden Bird Story:
Once upon a time, in a faraway kingdom, a king longed for a golden bird with a melodious song. 
He offered great riches to whoever could find it, and his sons embarked on separate journeys. 
The youngest, a kind prince, met a clever fox who guided him. They found the bird, but just as the 
prince was about to catch it, the bird flew away, leaving the prince with only the fox's promise to share his reward. 

Moral Lesson:
The story highlights the importance of kindness, as the prince was willing to share with the fox, and the 
consequences of greed, as the king's desire for the bird overshadowed the joy it brought to others.
